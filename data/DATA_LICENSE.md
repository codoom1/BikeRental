## Capital Bikeshare data

The processed dataset incorporates Capital Bikeshare trip-history data.

Users must review and comply with the complete agreement. This summary is
provided for convenience and does not replace its terms.

Source: [Capital Bikeshare System Data](https://capitalbikeshare.com/system-data)

Those observations remain subject to the provider's applicable terms. No
